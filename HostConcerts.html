HOST THE BBNV 

What the band can do for you!

We regularly perform at churches, schools, adult living centers, museums, outdoor venues, and private buisnesses with seating for 500 or more. We can perform at many hosted venues for free, and we can turn the event into a fundraiser for our host as well!

DOWNLOAD PDF TO LEARN MORE

Churches

Provide a wonderful event for your congregation

Many churches spend thousands of dollars to bring in musicians to perform with their music programs.  We can give our host church complimentary tickets to provide a free concert to partitioners while allowing us to bring music lovers into your church.  

Schools

Have our musicians work with your students

Do you want to have an easy fundraiser?  A free masterclass with world-renown musicians? and host an event that increases community appreciation for live performance?  We will host a masterclass with your band program, and give you 20% of ticket sales if you host a concert with us!  

Other Venues

We are looking to build relationships and have creative concert locations

We are creating a loyal fanbase that loves to travel and seek out new experiences.  If you own a venue that seats a band of 35, and an audience of over 500, we could perform a concert at your location.  From nonprofit organizations like museums, or city halls, to for-profit businesses like breweries and wineries, a collaboration can boost both our groups.
